My Future Aspirations

Becoming a Data Analyst

I aspire to be a data analyst, which is why I'm studying software engineering with this focus. I want to combine technical skills with analytical thinking to solve real problems.

Why Data Analytics?

I love helping in contemporary issues such as social relations. Data analytics lets me understand social patterns, identify problems, and suggest solutions based on evidence, not assumptions.

Short-term Goals (Next 1-2 years)

  • Complete my Software Engineering degree with strong data analysis projects
  • Master React and front-end development through practical projects
  • Build a portfolio of data analysis case studies
  • Contribute to open-source projects, especially my weather dashboard
  • Learn advanced statistics and data visualization techniques

Long-term Vision

I want to work on projects that "enable making grand decisions to solve world problems." Data should help us understand social issues, environmental challenges, and economic patterns.

Areas of Interest

Social Data Analysis Project Management Predictive Modeling Human behavior

What I'm Curious About

How can data help us understand and improve social relationships? What patterns exist in how communities interact? How can technology bridge gaps in education and opportunity?

Continuous Growth

The journey from "blank on where to start" to "going somewhere with software engineering" continues. Every day is about learning, building, and preparing to use data for meaningful impact.
